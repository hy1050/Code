RESEARCH AND SIMULATE SPEED SIGN RECOGNITION SYSTEM IN SELF-DRIVING CAR

Theoretical foundations of Model

This research focuses on developing a speed sign recognition system in self-driving cars by combining computer vision methods and deep learning. Utilizing techniques such as image processing and convolutional neural networks (CNNs), the system is trained on a large dataset to ensure accurate and reliable sign detection. Subsequently, the system's performance is evaluated in a simulated environment, providing crucial insights for the future development of autonomous vehicle technology, thereby enhancing safety and efficiency in automated transportation.
